Are you tired of long queues?
Start queuing without having to be physically in the place and only come when your turn is almost there, isn’t that beautiful?

Queue On the application is for a new waiting method instead of waiting inside the restaurant for the order or Caffe or even for an event.

You simply put the invoice number and scan the merchant QR code and you are free to go anywhere and the app will notify you once the order or your line is ready.
What does Queue on App?
Queue On App has developed by taking into consideration of today’s business requirements of managing the customers in a proper and civilized manner.
The Queue on App can help restaurants and retailers avoid overcrowding and long queues during and after the COVID pandemic and organize them.
